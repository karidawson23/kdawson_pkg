Dictionary explaining the meaning of all of the column names
- Sample: is the identification code for each sample
- Date : shows the date of the sampling
- Day : redundant date column
- Time : time of encounter
- e2 : concentration of e2 in the turtle plasma
- t : concentration of testosterone in turtle plasma
- vtg : concentration of plasma vtg
- Output : number of eggs laid by female turtle
- Curved carapace length : measurment in cm of the curved carapace length
- Straight carapace length : measurment in cm of the straight carapace length
